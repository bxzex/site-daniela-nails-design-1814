Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?
While we do accept walk-ins when availability permits, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure you receive your preferred time and technician.
What products do you use?
We use only premium, industry-leading brands for our polishes, gels, and acrylics. We prioritize products that are long-lasting and safe for your natural nails.
How long do gel extensions last?
With proper care, our gel extensions typically last between 3 to 4 weeks before needing a fill. We provide aftercare instructions to help maintain your beautiful nails.
Do you offer custom nail art?
Yes! Our technicians are highly skilled artists. Feel free to bring in reference photos, and we will work with you to create a custom design you'll love.
